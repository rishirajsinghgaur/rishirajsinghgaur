who i am

PhD scholar at Manipal University Jaipur, working on data deduplication for Industrial IoT — where machine learning, blockchain audit trails, and federated learning meet. My frameworks are evaluated on the TEP, SKAB, and SWaT benchmarks, with a focus on deduplication that never destroys the rare anomaly signatures that matter most.

Alongside research, I work as a Senior Process Associate in the Office of the Controller of Examinations at MUJ, automating academic processes and large-scale data workflows, and I train staff in digital skills at MUJ TEC. PhD supervised by Dr. Linesh Raja.
